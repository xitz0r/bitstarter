Don't you wish you could travel to the future and speak to yourself? Now you can! Well.... kinda! Until time travel isn't achieved, you can rely on Hello, future me! Either if you're happy and want to remind your future you, how to stay that way, or if you're going throught a rought path and want to show your future you, how strong you were, Hello, Future me! is your thing! You may still learn a thing or two... you'll be able to think Wow, back in those days I was really stupid!, or maybe.... That is so weird! Where are all my wrinkles?. And who knows you'll say OH MAN, IS THAT REALLY ME?!?.

Well, all jokes aside, with Hello, future me! you'll be able to upload a letter, or even a video, and receive it a day, a month, a year, or even 10 years later! Once you upload it, you'll lose access to it, and will only receive it when the time has come. So maybe it's better for you to forget all about it, because depending on the time you specified, it can take a while! But it will be totally worth it! You can upload how many videos and letters you want, each of them with a different waiting time, just remember, you will lose complete access to those uploaded files, otherwise it wouldn't be a surprise!
